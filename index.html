Affirmative

Subject + have/has + been + gerund
I have been working
You have been working
He/she/it has been working
We have been working
You have been working
They have been working

Negative

Subject + haven't/hasn't + been + gerund
I haven't been cooking
You haven't been cooking
He/she/it hasn't been cooking
We haven't been cooking
You haven't been cooking
They haven't been cooking

Questions

Have/has + subject + been + gerund
Have I been crying?
Have you been crying?
Has he/she/it been crying?
Have we been crying?
Have you been crying?
Have they been crying?
